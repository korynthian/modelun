More links

Research

  • World Factbook - extremely helpful tool for initial nation research
  • Wikipedia - Great for getting mostly accurate info easily, but verify with more accurate sources for citations. Wikipedia's source list can be a great place to find sources!
  • News sites - make sure they're reliable, and check for biases (e.g. news sites from Russia would be state-biased and may not report accurate information). Sites such as BBC, Washington Post, Forbes, Wall Street Journal, etc. are reliable and will report facts - it may still feature bias, but will not report lies

Position Papers

  • All American MUN's Position Paper Outline - Instructions on writing position papers - you don't need to follow this to the letter, but can be helpful to get started
  • Wisemee Positon Paper Guide - Some more tips on position paper writing

Resolutions

  • Wisemme How to Write a MUN Resolution - Guidelines, overview, and outline on writing working/resolution papers

Speaking

  • Wisemee Points and Motions - Tips on how to use motions and speak during conferences

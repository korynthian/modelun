What is Model UN Chair Tools?

MUN Chair Tools is made by Victor Kisselev for Lake Braddock Secondary School Middle School Model United Nation club. It is designed to replace clunky solutions like Google Docs which leave chairs taking half an hour to sort out technical issues and pausing committee to find the highlight button.

Tips & Tricks

  • Hitting the ESC (escape) key closes any dialog.
  • The default topic, when no topic is entered, is "Solutions"
  • Don't forget to add countries!
